ALTERNATIVE VEGETABLE OILS

Palm oil is an incredibly efficient crop, producing more oil per land area than any other equivalent oil crop. Globally, palm oil supplies 40% of the world’s vegetable oil demand on just under 6% of the land to produce all vegetable oils. To get the same amount of alternative oils like soybean, coconut, or oil you would need anything between 4 and 10 times more land, which would just shift the problem to other of the world and threaten other habitats, species and communities. Furthermore, there are millions of farmers who depend on producing palm oil for their livelihoods. Boycotting palm oil is not the answer. , we need to demand more action to tackle the issues and go further and faster.

Source: Our planet
